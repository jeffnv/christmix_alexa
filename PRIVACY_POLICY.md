# Privacy Policy for Christ Mix Radio Alexa Skill

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the Christ Mix Radio Alexa skill ("the Skill") handles information when you use it on your Alexa-enabled device.

## Information Collection

The Christ Mix Radio skill does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The skill only streams audio content from https://christmix.gay/stream.

## Data Usage

- The skill does not require any personal information to function
- No user data is collected or stored
- No analytics or tracking is performed
- The skill operates entirely through voice commands to control audio playback

## Third-Party Services

The skill streams audio content from an external source (https://christmix.gay/stream). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of this third-party streaming service.

## Children's Privacy

The skill does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the skill's support channels.
